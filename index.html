Welcome to the Puzzle Exchange!

Exchange puzzles you no longer want to build. Join us for a coffee and a hangout!

About us

Each month we meet up at a local coffee shop in Cape Town to exchange our puzzle sets with each other.

Think of it like a bookclub for puzzles!

We often like to setup a puzzle build for the afternoon. It's relaxed, it's fun. Sometimes we finish, sometimes we don't!

Our Story

It all started with two friends who enjoyed building puzzles together.

If you are a puzzle lover like us, you no doubt have collected a few sets over the years. Some of us like to frame our creations, others break theirs up and keep on their shelf for another time.

Instead of purchasing another, how about exchanging your set with someone else and give your puzzle a second life?

Why join us?

  1. Exchange puzzles with members (just like a bookclub!) and try out something new each month.
  2. Meet and connect with new people over a coffee.
  3. Discover a new and relaxing hobby to enjoy with others.

No puzzle? No problem!

If you’d simply like to enjoy a cup of something delicious and relax with friends, that’s great.

Each month during our exchanges we’ll have a puzzle ready to build for the afternoon and just hangout.
