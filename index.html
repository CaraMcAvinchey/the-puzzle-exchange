Welcome to the Puzzle Exchange


Exchange puzzles you no longer want to build.

About us

Each month we meet up at a local coffee shop in Cape Town to exchange our puzzle sets with each other and we often like to setup a puzzle build for the afternoon.

Our Story

It all started with two friends who enjoyed building puzzles together. If you are a puzzle lover like us, you no doubt have collected a few sets over the years. Instead of purchasing more, how about exchanging your set with a fellow puzzle builder?

Why join us?

No puzzle? No problem! If you’d simply like to enjoy a cup of something delicious and relax with friends, that’s great. Each month during our exchanges we’ll have a puzzle ready to build for the afternoon and just hangout.
